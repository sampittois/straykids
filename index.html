You make Stray Kids STAY

Stray Kids (스트레이 키즈) is a South Korean boy group under JYP Entertainment. The group consists of 8: Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N. Stray Kids was created through the survival program with the same name, Stray Kids. Stray Kids debuted on March 25, 2018. As of February 10th, 2022, they are also signed under Republic Records. Their fandom name is Stay.
